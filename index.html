About F1

This website provides comprehensive information about the world of Formula 1, from driver profiles and race schedules to standings and results, to the latest news on teams and F1 technology. Designed for racing fans, this website provides an engaging and accessible experience following the Formula 1 season.

Formula 1 is the world's most prestigious motor racing series, showcasing cutting-edge technology and the best drivers from various countries. Races are held on various international circuits, featuring high speeds and complex strategies.
